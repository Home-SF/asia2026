Upload a location export from Google or Apple and it'll show up here as your own colored trail, alongside everyone's Check In pins from the home page.

How to get your export

Google: if Location History / Timeline is on in the Google Maps app, go to takeout.google.com and export "Location History (Timeline)" as JSON.

Apple: no simple built-in export — use the Google Maps app (works on iPhone) or any GPX-exporting tracking app.

Only points within the trip dates (Dec 19, 2026 – Jan 1, 2027) are kept.
